Abour AlexRudn

Alex was born in 1983 in Moscow Russia in a family of Chemical Engineer and Phys-Chemical Engineer. She is from the third generation of working educated women in her family. Her maternity grandmother was School Principal and chemistry teacher. Growing up the only child during perestroika left a lot of space for creativity and free play. One of the brightest memories from her childhood is the attendance of contemporary sculptures exhibition at Krymsky Val in 1986. Since then Alex fall in love with shapes and colors and all kind of art expressions including paintings, installations, sculptures, and mobile sculptures. In 1999 Alex graduated from High school. In 2005 She received BS & MS in environmental engineering and in 2010 she graduated from Univercity of Massachusetts Boston with PhD in computational chemistry.
